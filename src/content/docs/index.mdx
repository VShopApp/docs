# About

VShop is a mobile app for iOS & Android which makes it possible to check your Valorant store as easy as it can be. We believe in privacy. Therefore, we do not store any data on our servers. All data is stored locally on your device. For more information, see our documentation under Security.

For additional support, please join our Discord server.

If you have general problems with your game (with payments for example) please contact Riot Games directly.
